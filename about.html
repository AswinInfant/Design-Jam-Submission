The Donutery Story

2016

Our story begins with a small kitchen
in our backyard where we tested our
culinary skills and found passion in doughnuts.
As we worked to create the most innovative
and delicious doughnuts around, we invited
friends and family to weekly taste-test gatherings
and shared fresh doughnuts with our neighbours.

2018

Our flagship franchise opened in Dec 2018
setting the stage for our community-driven brand
dedicated to the freshest doughnuts and absolute
best customer service. As we expanded our
franchise, we brought with us our
core values of quality, connection & delight.

2019

Over the years, we built upon our mission
to always create places where people
could connect and experience the delight
of our doughnuts. And now we are on a mission
to spread our word of Doughnuts, so that
everyone can experience the love
and taste of our Doughnuts

Crafting Fresh Doughnuts!

Here at The Donutery, we infuse an exciting, soulful
energy into the art of doughnut making,
and stand by our commitment to serve
Fresh Doughnuts!We believe that the only
way to enjoy a doughnut is hot and fresh,
paired with the perfect cup of coffee,
and served with a smile.

We make our doughnuts daily, from scratch,
using only the finest ingredients possible,
and prepare as much as we can in house.
Our team works day in and day out,
mixing and rolling out hand-crafted dough,
standing over simmering pots of
fruit purees and sizzling pans of chocolate,
and whipping up fresh creams and custards.

We fry in small batches all day long,
guaranteeing each customer a fresh-out-of-
the-fryer treat. From the moment you step
into one of our shops and bite into one of our
hand-crafted doughnuts, you’ll surely agree
that Donutery is not your ordinary
doughnut experience!

We can't wait to share our deliciousdoughnuts and coffee
with you soon!
