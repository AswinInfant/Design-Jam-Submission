Forum Vijaya Mall

Vadapalani

The Donutery franchise was first established at Forum Vijaya Mall on December 2018. Located at the food court of the mall, our shop celebrates the city's rich industrial history, with a space designed to be reminiscent of a doughnut factory.From the rolling of the handmade dough, to the mixing of house made glazes, custards and creams, visitors can watch the fresh doughnuts being made from start-to-finish through large windows that peer into the kitchen.

VR Chennai

Anna Nagar West

The Second Donutery franchise was established at VR Chennai on September 2019.Our flagship shop is designed to feel like it’s in an old city building - outfitted with antique fixtures, vintage wallpaper and hand-painted tile. Watch us make our hand-crafted doughnuts from start-to-finish thanks to an open kitchen and front-and-center prep spaces.Located at the popular location, this shop quickly won the hearts of San Diegans thanks to our made-fresh-hourly doughnuts
